    Automatic Speech Recognition and Natural Language Processing (OPT5) #

    Teaching #

    Kim Gerdes and Marc Evrard

    Class hours #

    Friday, 2:00 pm to 5:15 pm (nov-dec)

    Prerequisite #

    • Foundational Principles of Machine Learning (TC0)
    • Hands on Natural Language Processing (TC3)

    Summary #

    Introductory class on speech and natural language processing. The class is organized in a series of seminars proposed by researchers in the field.

    Outline #

    • Seminar 1:
      • General Introduction (Kim Gerdes)
      • From Linguistics to Natural Language Processing (Ioana Vasilescu)
    • Seminar 2: Treebanks and Oral Syntax (Kim Gerdes)
    • Seminar 3: NLP, semantic, mutlimots (Agata Savary)
    • Seminar 4: Voice Recognition (Claude Barras and Marc Evrard)
    • Seminar 5: Automatic Emotion Recognition (Laurence Devillers)
    • Seminar 6: Speech Interaction (Samir Bennacef)
    • Seminar 7: Conclusion, Preparation of Paper Reading Assignment (Kim Gerdes and Marc Evrard)

    Assessment #

    • Session 1: 40% attendance, 60% CCTP1 (ASR Practical + Paper Reading)
    • Session 2: 40% attendance, 60% CCTP2 (ASR Practical + Paper Reading)
